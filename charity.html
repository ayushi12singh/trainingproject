GIVING BACK TO OUR

"THE GIVING DRINK"

Each month at COFFEE HOUSE we create a special drink feature to give back to our community. A portion of each Giving Drink sold will go to a local charity, and is a fun way for us to feature something new to you while giving back to our community! Ask your barista for our current months special the next time you join us!

OUR CHARITY PARTNERS

COFFEE HOUSE’s Giving Drink program celebrates our commitment to local Las Vegas. We’ve partnered with over a 18 local charities since opening the Durango location in 2019, including local public schools, animal rescues, children’s charities, and mental health advocacy groups. We’re proud to continue supporting the community that supports our team.
